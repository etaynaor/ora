COP30 Hear the Rainforest

COP30 • Hear the Rainforest

Concept

For the COP30 Climate Conference in Belém, Brazil (November 10–21, 2025), create a live musical concert based on real-time sounds from the Amazon rainforest—rain, birds, insects, and even tree-cutting.

This project will use Oracle's robust data infrastructure and AI to translate the planet's pulse into a moving auditory experience.

COP30 • Hear the Rainforest

Execution

  • Deploy a network of acoustic sensors throughout the rainforest, streaming data to Oracle Cloud.
  • Feed live audio streams into a generative music AI model (developed and run on Oracle platforms) to produce a dynamic, real-time musical composition.
  • Collaborate with a renowned conductor or musician to guide and enhance the AI-generated composition.
  • A remote choir receives daily AI-generated musical scores and performs them.
  • The performance is accompanied by live visuals from the Amazon rainforest, streamed via Oracle's infrastructure.

COP30 • Hear the Rainforest

Impact

Daily musical performances powerfully communicating the state of the rainforest, highlighting Oracle’s commitment to sustainability and innovation.

  • Provides a unique, emotionally resonant voice for a critical environmental issue at a global forum.
  • Showcases Oracle's technology as a tool for positive global change and data-driven storytelling.
  • Generates significant international media coverage and positions Oracle as a thought leader.
